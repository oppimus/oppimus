Can you tell me if I qualify for the Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC)?

+
Yes — we review your income, filing status, and qualifying children as part of your intake and run it against current IRS rules. You can also preview using the official IRS EITC Assistant tool.

Can I file with an ITIN instead of a Social Security number?

+
Absolutely. We frequently help immigrants and non-citizens file legally using ITINs and guide you through all required documentation and IRS procedures.

What documents do I need to get started?

+
W-2s or 1099s, last year's return if available, your ID, children's information, and any deduction receipts. Not sure what you have? Just start the intake — we'll send a simple checklist within 24 hours.

How fast can I get my refund?

+
Many e-filed returns with direct deposit arrive in about 1–3 weeks depending on the IRS. We help you set up tracking and manage expectations throughout the process.

Is my information safe and confidential?

+
Yes. All documents are handled with bank-grade encryption. We are completely judgment-free — your data is used only to prepare and file your return, nothing else.

When is my exact fee confirmed?

+
After your free intake, we review your situation and confirm your exact flat fee before beginning any work. No surprise charges — ever. Zero cost to explore your options.
